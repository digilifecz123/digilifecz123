Hi there, so glad to have you here!

Friends know me as Tuan Phung, a self-taught front end developer, who can use lines of codes to create wonders. It is my burning passion to explore hot tech innovations and the world of entrepreneurship. Oh, and one more thing... I love my Macbook... maybe a little too much? Hah.

In my past few years, I have been enthusiastically working in the tech/sales industry, as a Key Account Manager at Webmium and as a Business Sales Consultant at Vodafone. Those were exciting places to be at, but I believe the tech industry has more to offer and so I am moving on…

Today, I am walking around, searching for a job as a front end developer. I would love to sharpen up my geek skills and unleash the living potential in me. Because I strongly believe, with passion and diligence, I have what it takes to become a professional in this field.

Look closely! All you see on this site is the fruitful outcome of those sleepless nights, where I applied all my self-taught knowledge about web development.

Feel free to check out some of my work. If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to contact me!

Front end development
